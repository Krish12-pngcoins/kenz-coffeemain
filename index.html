A Canton Hidden Gem

"Kenz" Means Treasure

Founded by co-owners Abdulnaser Alnajjar and Moshtak Alamri, Kenz Coffee House is a cozy haven designed for connection. We transformed a space on Cherry Hill Road into an elegant, welcoming environment with crisp interior design—perfect for catching up, studying, or unwinding.

We pride ourselves on authenticity. Our coffee is freshly ground and carefully prepared. Whether you are craving the traditional spice profiles of authentic Yemeni brews, looking for our renowned Spanish Coffee that tastes like a Latin American dessert in a cup, or seeking a perfect balance of frothy milk in our classic lattes, you are in good hands.

Kenz is deeply embedded in the Canton community. We want to be a treasure for our neighbors—a place where the atmosphere is always inviting, the service is impeccable, and every cup is poured with intention.
