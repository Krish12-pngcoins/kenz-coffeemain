Our Story

A Treasure of the Canton Community

Founded by co-owners Abdulnaser Alnajjar and Moshtak Alamri, Kenz Coffee House is a transformation of space and spirit. We turned a former physical therapy office on Cherry Hill Road into a stylish, welcoming café designed for community connection.

Our coffee beans are imported directly from Yemen — the historical birthplace of coffee. Every cup honors centuries of tradition, bringing rich, earthy flavors right here to Michigan. To ensure transparency, every item on our menu is priced with tax included, so there are no surprises at checkout.

We are deeply committed to the Canton community. From sponsoring the Canton High School football and soccer teams to providing beverages for local organizations, we want "Kenz" — which means treasure in Arabic — to truly be a treasure for our neighbors.

When you visit, you'll often find us mingling with guests to ensure every experience is perfect. Our promise: If your coffee isn't right, we'll brew you another one on the spot.

"Kenz" means treasure.

We pour that philosophy into every organic brew, every fresh pastry, and every smile shared across the counter. Whether you are hosting a book club or grabbing a quick morning latte, you are welcome here.
